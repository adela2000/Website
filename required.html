A-G Requirements

Social Studies/Science

CSU: 2 years (including 1 year of U.S. history or one-half year of U.S. history one-half year of civics or American government; and 1 year of world history, cultures, and geography

UC: 2 years (including 1 year of U.S. history or one-half year of U.S. history one-half year of civics or American government; and 1 year of other approved social studies/science

English

CSU: 4 years (including no more than 1 year of ESL/ELD courses)

UC: 4 years (including no more than 1 year of ESL/ELD courses)

Mathematics

CSU: 3 years of mathematics ( including algebra I and II, geometry); 4 years recommended *

UC: 3 years of mathematics ( including algebra I and II, geometry); 4 years recommended *

Science

CSU: 2 years (1 year of physical science and 1 year of biological science)

UC: 2 years (must include at least 2 of the 3 foundational subjects of biology, chemistry, and physics); 3 years recommended *

Foreign Language

CSU: 2 years of language other than English (must be the same language, American Sign Language accepted) *

UC: 2 years of language other than English (must be the same language, American Sign Language accepted) ; 3 years recommended *

Visual and Performing Arts

CSU: 1 yearlong course in visual and performing arts (selected from dance, music, theatre/drama and visual arts)

UC: 1 yearlong course in visual and performing arts (selected from dance, music, theatre/drama and visual arts)

Elective

CSU: 1 year of an elective chosen from any area on approved “a-g” course list

UC: 1 year of an elective chosen from any area on approved “a-g” course list
